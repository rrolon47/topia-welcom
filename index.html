WELCOME TO THE PARTY!

How to move:

When your cursor is a "shoe print:, click anywhere on the map to move to that spot.
If you can't move anymore you probably hit the edge of the map.

Interact with things:

If your cursor turns into a "pointing hand", you can interact with that object.

How to sit:

Hover over your avatar, when a "chair" appears click to sit down.

How to go to another world:

To the left of your screen you will see a "globe" icon. Click on that, then go to the "Recent" tab to navigate to a previous location.

How to send a message

There is an "envelope" icon on the video screen of those around you. Click on that to message that person.

To the left of your screen you will see an "envelope" icon. Click on that to view your inbox.
